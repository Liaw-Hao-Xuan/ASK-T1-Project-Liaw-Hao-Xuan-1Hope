Soundtrack

Explore a small carousel of memorable tracks that capture the mood of the games.

Once Upon a Time

The first track in the whole game which you hear during the opening story.

Ghost Fight

A track that plays when encountering the ghost, napstablook.

Heartache

A theme that plays when encountering the first major boss. The queen of the underground.

Bonetrousle

A theme that plays when facing the first bone brother Papyrus.

Metal Crusher

Another boss theme that plays when encounter the killer robot named Mettaton.

Bergentrucking

The theme that plays when encountering the king, ASGORE. The end of your journey has come.

Battle Against A True Hero

A theme that only plays during the genocide route requiring you to take the lives of many monsters. Undyne stands in your way preventing you from continuing.

Power Of "NEO"

The second phase of Mettaton only seen during the genocide route.

Megalovania

The most iconic and recognizable theme in the game. Used by the second bone brother, Sans. Only seen during the genocide route.

Hopes and Dreams

Theme only seen in the pacifist route used by the son of the king, named Asriel taking the power of the 6 human souls attemping to steal yours.
